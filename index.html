About Us

Welcome to Food Hut

Nestled in the heart of Chandigarh, our restaurant was born from a passion for specific cuisine, culture, or cooking style, e.g., "authentic Italian flavors" or "modern fusion cuisine". Founded by Ritik Kumar, we believe in creating more than just a meal; we’re crafting unforgettable dining experiences.

At Food Hut, we are dedicated to using the freshest, locally sourced ingredients to bring you vibrant flavors and beautifully plated dishes. Our chefs pour their creativity and expertise into each dish, blending tradition with innovation to surprise and delight your taste buds.

We invite you to join us, savor our signature dishes, and enjoy our warm, welcoming atmosphere—whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply indulging in great food with friends.

Since opening our doors, Food Hut has been dedicated to sharing the flavors and warmth of Cuisine Origin with our guests. From beloved family recipes to cherished culinary traditions, each dish is prepared with love, just as our family has done for generations. Welcome to our table.
